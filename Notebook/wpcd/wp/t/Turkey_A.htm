SOS Children in Turkey

The charity began negotiations with regard to the construction of a community in Turkey in the 1970s. It was, however, not before 1987 that an agreement between a private Turkish children's foundation "Türkiye Korunmaya Muhtac Cocuklar Vakfi" (Foundation for the Protection of Children) and SOS Children was signed. A suitable plot of land was found in Bolluça, about 35 km from Istanbul, and the construction work started in autumn 1988. In spring 1992, the construction was completed and the first children moved into their new home.

After the severe earthquakes of August 1999, SOS Children's Bolluça community expanded its capacity in order to help more children in need. In early 2001 the renovation works on the SOS Children's Bolluça site started. Since suitable flats to serve as a SOS Youth Facility could not be found yet, the youths were temporarily accommodated in the aunt's house on the site. Despite some skepticism regarding the work of SOS Children on the part of the population in the area in the beginning, a good relationship meanwhile was established with the neighbourhood.

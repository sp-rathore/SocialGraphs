National Giving Week - giving hope to orphans

13/10/2006

Whether it’s a present for a friend or a gift remembering a loved one, sponsorship is the ideal way to show that you are giving hope to orphans worldwide.

National Giving Week, which this year is 16-22 October 2006, means that people up and down the country can make a positive change for the future.

Why not take part in the charity challenge? Simply empty out your cupboards, lofts and garages and let the proceeds from eBay benefit children in need. Donating up to 100% of the proceeds to SOS Children means we will be able to care for even more children with your support.

For more information on the Give it up for Charity Challenge to help raise money for SOS Children, please visit http://www.missionfish.org.uk/About/mfnews/mfn_290806.jsp

Don’t forget pass this onto your friends and be part of the Give it up for Charity Challenge!

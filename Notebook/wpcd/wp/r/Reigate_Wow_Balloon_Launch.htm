World Orphan Week Reigate Balloon Launch

Despite the terrible weather, the pupils of Dunottar School for Girls in Reigate were extremely excited to take part in the World Orphan Week balloon launch on 6 October.

The girls show off their sponsored balloons

The pupils brave the elements to release their balloons

And they're off!

The pupils and staff of Dunottar school cheer the balloons on their way, hoping that their balloon will travel the furthest and win them a prize. There's also a prize for the finder of the winning balloon, so keep your eyes peeled!

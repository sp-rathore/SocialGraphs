Middle East Crisis: News updates

27/07/2006

The ongoing conflict in the Middle East has left hundreds dead and many thousands more homeless or refugees. With a number of villages and outreach programmes in the region, SOS Children has so far been fortunate to avoid any losses, and is extending its support to the emergency relief programmes in the surrounding areas.

SOS Children is currently active in:

  • Israel

  • Lebanon

  • Palestinian Territories

  • Syria

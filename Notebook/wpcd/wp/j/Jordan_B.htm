Jordan Aspinall writes brilliant book

Jordan Aspinall (aged 7) is a budding author who has put his talents to good use for our cause. Inspired by a cuddly toy, he has written and drawn a beautiful story book "The Adventures of little Honey Bunny" which was sold for £1 a copy to raise funds for SOS Children's Villages.

Jordan and his book

You can download a copy of Jordan's book here: Jordan's book, it is well worth reading, and a very good way to fundraise. Jordan and his family sponsor a child in Jordan (appropriately enough!) and said they were all happy to help others not as fortunate as themselves.

Relevant Countries: Jordan.

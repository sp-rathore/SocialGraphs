Link to our web site

If you have a home page or other web page we would very much appreciate you linking to this website. Links are valuable to us and bring us income at no cost to you.

You could, for example, use one of the following HTML syntax:

A <a ref="http://www.sos-uk.org.uk/children-charity.htm">children charity</a> I support.
or
<a ref="http://www.sos-uk.org.uk/children-charity.htm">SOS Children</a>

Alternatively you can place one of the banners we have created for you. Just choose your favourite banner and download it directly onto your PC. Please don't forget to link the banner with the URL http://www.sos-uk.org.uk/children-charity.htm . To download the banner, move the mouse over the graphic, click with the right mouse button and save the picture of the banner on your hard drive!

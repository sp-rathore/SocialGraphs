Photographic Expression

The flowers look very beautiful together with all my friends and brothers and sisters. When I am with them I feel really good ... when I have problems they help me.
Dalina, f, 12 years old; Colombia

(Las flores se ven así muy lindas con todos mis amigos y mi hermano Cuando estoy con ellos me siento muy bien ... cuando tengo dificultades ellos me ayudan
Dalina, f, 12 años)

All these photographs and text come from research conducted by SOS Children's Village Hermann Gmeiner Academy. Copyright is reserved and no unauthorized use permitted.

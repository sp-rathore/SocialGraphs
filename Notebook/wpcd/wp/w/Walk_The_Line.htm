Charity Preview: Walk the Line

15/01/2006

Johnny Cash was a committed supporter of SOS Children and a neighbour to The SOS Children’s Village Barrett Town, Jamaica.

On Wednesday 1 February at 7.30pm, SOS Children is hosting a preview of the critically acclaimed Johnny Cash biopic, ‘Walk the Line’ at Cineworld Cambridge. Tickets are a snip at just £6 - with every penny going to the world wide work of SOS Children. There will be a raffle with the chance to win Johnny Cash memorabilia and a six-month cinema pass, before the film rolls at 8pm.

To reserve your tickets now, please call SOS Children on 01223 365589 between 9 am and 5 pm weekdays. Payments can be made by credit or debit card.

The film, featuring Joaquin Phoenix and Reese Witherspoon, has won three Golden Globes and has been described as ‘Flawless’ and ‘Extraordinary’ by critics.

We hope to see you there!

If you wish to support our ongoing work for orphans everywhere, then you could become a sponsor or regular donor.

Relevant Countries: Jamaica.

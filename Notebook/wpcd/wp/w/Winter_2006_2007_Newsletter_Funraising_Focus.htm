08/01/2007

Fundraising Focus

Across the UK
Out and About

Flora London Marathon 2007
After the success of last year we would love to build on the number of people running in the 2007 London Marathon (Sunday 22 April). If you are lucky enough to have a place please contact Caroline on 01223 365589 or email her at caroline@soschildren.org to see how you can support SOS Children.

Cycling Event

Doug Brain cycled 7000km in 70 days to Dakar, Senegal, raising thousands of pounds for AIDS projects in Africa.

Visit Doug's website at www.aid-by-cycle.co.uk

In your area

Volunteer Regional Co-ordinators

The number of our regional volunteers has increased but we would still like to see more people who can give about 9-12 hours a week. We welcome people from across the UK but especially if you live in Wales, Scotland or Northern Ireland. We would like to develop the volunteer network in these areas - for more details please contact Rebecca Cook on 01223 365589 or email rebeccac@soschildren.org

Cashback Rewards www.cashback-rewards.org.uk
For your online shopping needs you can use Cashback rewards and save up your reward points for SOS Children - it could be as much as £50 .

Everyclick www.everyclick.com

Not sure which search engine to use on the world wide web? Everyclick.com supports SOS Children with a percentage being donated to us depending on the number of searches.

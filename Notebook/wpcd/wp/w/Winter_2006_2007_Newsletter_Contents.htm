05/01/2007

We are pleased to welcome you to the Winter edition of our quarterly newsletter.

In this newsletter you will find:

  • a letter from the Chief Executive summarising the events of the last three months

  • a report from the perspective of a child in The Gambia

  • an article on the New skills for a new life project in Nigeria

  • news on our partnership with HSBC and theFuture First project

  • the latest news from around the world in our Stop Press

  • an insight into the brilliant fundraising work of our supporters in the Fundraising Focus

  • descriptions of some of the projects that have been funded in the 'A year of charity fundraising'

  • a focus on the situation on Romania in our article'Change a child’s life today'.

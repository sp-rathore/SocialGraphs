2006 Charity Christmas cards for SOS Children

01/10/2006

Here are the designs we have chosen for our Christmas cards this year. As always, a high proportion of the price of these cards is a direct donation to our charity.

If you would like to place an order for any of our Christmas cards you can:

  • email cards@soschildrensvillages.org.uk

  • call us on 01223 365589

  • place your order online at http://www.christmas-cards.org.uk/sos/

Postage

Postage for one pack of cards costs £1.50, two to four packs £2.50, five to seven packs £5.50, eight to 10 packs £6.50, and for more than 10 packs, postage is free. The maximum time for delivery is 21 days.

Skating Bears

Pack of 10 cards, 168 x 113 mm, £4.99. The message inside says "With best wishes for Christmas and the New Year".

Starry Night

Pack of 10 cards, 112 x 118 mm, £3.99. The message inside reads "Season's Greetings".

Sprouts

A pack of 10 cards, 113 x 119 mm, £3.99. The message inside says "Season's Greetings".

On Christmas Day

Pack of 8 cards, 127 x 93 mm, £2.99. The message inside says "With best wishes for Christmas and the New Year".

Snowdrop

Pack of 8 cards, 93 x 126 mm, £2.99. The message inside says "Season's Greetings".

Travelling Musicians

Pack of 10 cards, 185 x 139 mm, £4.99. The message inside reads "With best wishes for Christmas and the New Year".

Description

Message from a REAL cell biologist: This is simply awful. There is virtually nothing correct. The author has absolutely no idea about cell biology. The Golgi is a typical example, completely wrong. It isn't anything like the drawing you have made. This is an example of the power of the imagination over a limited ability to deal with the facts. I am disappointed that such an awful mess should make its way into Wikimedia.

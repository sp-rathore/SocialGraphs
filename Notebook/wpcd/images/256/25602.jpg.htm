Sailors await the arrival of the Military Sealift Command (MSC) hospital ship USNS Comfort (T-AH-20) way to aid victims of Hurricane Katrina as it prepares to make port in Naval Station Mayport, Fla., to take on supplies on their way to aid victims of Hurricane Katrina.

Background notes: Hospital ships

English: A hospital ship is a ship designated for primary function as a medical treatment facility or hospital; most are operated by the military forces or navies of various countries around the world.

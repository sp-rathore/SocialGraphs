Route map of the Dakota, Minnesota and Eastern Railroad and the Iowa, Chicago and Eastern Railroad as of 2002

State and lake outlines extracted from Perry-Castañeda Library Map Collection; the Library's FAQ asserts public domain status on the original map. Railroad outlines and city and state labels by Sean Lamb ( User:Slambo), January 20, 2005; created using Microsoft Paint, based on a map published in the May 2002 issue of Trains Magazine (p. 14).

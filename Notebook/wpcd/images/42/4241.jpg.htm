Description

General Dwight D. Eisenhower addresses American paratroopers on D-Day.

CREDIT: U.S. Army. "Dwight Eisenhower giving orders to American paratroopers in England." 1944 June 6. Prints and Photographs Division, Library of Congress.

U.S. Army photograph. No. SC 194399.

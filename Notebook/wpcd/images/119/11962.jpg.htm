Summary

This picture, view of YangJe-Cheon in Seoul, was taken by a Korean photographer, named Kim An-Soo.

I got a permission from his homepage about using this picture for Wikipedia.

Kim An-Soo's homepage: http://sajin.new21.net/

The residential towers in this picture are still undergoing its final stages of construction. The towers were completed in 2004.

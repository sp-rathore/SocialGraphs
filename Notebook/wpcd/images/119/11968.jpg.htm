Summary

Español: Edificios alrededor del World Trade Centre seriamente dañados por la caída de las torres gemelas

Ground Zero, New York City, N.Y. (Sept. 17, 2001) -- An aerial view shows only a small portion of the crime scene where the World Trade Centre collapsed following the Sept. 11 terrorist attack. Surrounding buildings were heavily damaged by the debris and massive force of the falling twin towers. Clean-up efforts continued for months. U.S. Navy photo by Chief Photographer's Mate Eric J. Tilford.

Sourced from the state archives of South Australia, in the State Library of South Australia.

The General Post Office and Clock Tower - Adelaide, 1950. The Town Hall is the lesser spire on the left of the photo. Running through Victoria square are F type drop centre trams of the Municipal Tramways Trust.

Entomologist Donald Barnard demonstrates the attraction of female yellow fever mosquitoes to his hand in an olfactometer. The olfactometer contains a screen separating the attractant (in this case, his hand) from the mosquitoes.

Photo by Peggy Greb.


This image is a work of the Agricultural Research Service, the research agency of the United States Department of Agriculture. As a work of the U.S. federal government, the image is in the public domain.

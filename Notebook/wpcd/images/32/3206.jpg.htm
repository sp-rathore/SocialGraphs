Clifton.bridge.longview.750pix.jpg‎ (750 × 491 pixel, file size: 69 KB, MIME type: image/jpeg)

The Clifton Suspension Bridge, spanning the Avon Gorge in Bristol. The Georgian terraces of Clifton can be seen on the right, with the district of Hotwells below them. On the left is Leigh Woods. Taken from a slip road off Brunel Way.
User:rbrwr took this photo and releases it under the GFDL.
Lightened, resized and repositioned by Adrian Pingstone in May 2003.

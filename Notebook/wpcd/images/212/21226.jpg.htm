Description

050722-N-9076-008 Waikiki Shell, Honolulu, Hawaii — Hip Hop artist Snoop Dogg pulls out all stops during a special concert for over 8000 military personnel and their dependents during the Bodog Salutes the Troops event. The two-day event honored the men and women of the United States Military Services, and benefited the military charity Fisher House Foundation. The event featured a prize-filled poker tournament and a comedy show and concert with celebrity and military attendance at both events.

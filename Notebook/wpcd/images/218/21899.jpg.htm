Summary

President of the United States George W. Bush, President of Mexico Vicente Fox, and Prime Minister of Canada Stephen Harper, right, stand in front of "El Castillo" in the Chichen Itza Archaeological Ruins on Thursday, March 30, 2006.

Credit: White House photo by Kimberlee Hewitt.

English: Soldiers raising the flag of Soviet Union on the roof of Reichstag building in Berlin, Germany in May, 1945. Photograph by Yevgeny Khaldei (1917-1997). Public Domain.

A picture of the rasing of the hammer and sickle flag over the Reichstag. The flag in this picture was made from a red tablecloth with the hammer and sickle themselves stamped on. It is not known whether the object missing in the edited version is a second wristwatch or a wrist compass. See also http://www.fahnenversand.de/fotw/flags/su%5Evctry.html and Image:Reichstag flag.jpg

Coin of Hermaeus, Indo-Greek king c.90-70 BCE.
Obv: Diademed bust of Hermaeus with Greek legend: BASILEOS SOTEROS ERMAIOU "Saviour King Hermaeus".
Rev: Zeus seated on throne, with scepter. Monogram. Kharoshti legend MAHARAJA TRATARASA HERMAYASA "Saviour King Hermaeus".
Actual size: 15mm, 2.03g.

Personal photograph, 2005. Released in the Public Domain.

Car bombing in Iraq, 2005.

Original caption:

ID: DASD0606834 Service Depicted: Army 050414A3240S026 Operation / Series: IRAQI FREEDOM As Coalition Forces respond to a car bombing in South Baghdad, Iraq (IRQ), a second car bomb is detonated, targeting those responding to the initial incident. The attack, aimed at the Iraqi police force, resulted in 18 casualties, two of which were police officers, during Operation IRAQI FREEDOM. Camera Operator: SPC RONALD SHAW JR., USA Date Shot: 14 Apr 2005

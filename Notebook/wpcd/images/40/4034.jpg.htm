This admittedly blurry photograph depicts a Map of Carib Territory on the Island of Dominica. The photo is too blurry to be used as such, but as it is still somewhat legible, I thought perhaps someone could at least use it for reference.

I, Gyrofrog, took this picture myself on May 7, 2005 at the National Museum of the American Indian during the Washington, D.C. Wikipedia Meetup.

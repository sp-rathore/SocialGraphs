This was the crest of Arsenal Football Club from c.1949 to 2002. In 2002, the club replaced the crest, stating in a press release:

"Principally, as the current crest incorporates many separate elements, which have been introduced over a number of years, there is uncertainty surrounding its exact origination. As a consequence, the Club is unable to copyright this crest."

The release is no longer available on the club website but a Google cache of it is here: . Another copy is here: . A BBC news story on the new crest is available here:

Since the club has admitted the crest is ineligible for copyright, it has been given the {{coatofarms}} image tag.

Español: bielorruso

The national flag of Belarus. New version replaces Zscout's "raster embedded in svg" image. The exact colours aren't officially defined; the colours used in this image are Pantone 1795C for red and 370C for green as estimated in Album des Pavillons (see en:Flag of Belarus).

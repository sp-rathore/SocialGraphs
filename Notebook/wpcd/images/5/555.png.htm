Summary

Created by Palffy.

The flag of Northern Ireland with a border.

Appended by M Dallas: This was a civil flag for Northern Ireland, but the status of this was abolished when the Belfast Stormont assembly was closed down in 1973. Thereafter, the Union Flag was made official for all purposes in Northern Ireland.

Summary

Death Valley visitors enjoying a record springtime wildflower bloom. Telescope Peak is in the background.

A record amount of rain at the best possible time during the winter caused what some have called the best bloom seen in 50 years. This area is located at the bottom of Jubilee Pass.

Photo taken by Neaco in March 2005.

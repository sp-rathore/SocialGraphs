Description

Flag of the Islamic Courts, according to this photograph.

This coincided with reported descriptions of the flag as a black flag with the en:Shahadah on it.

The top part of the encircled text above the crossed swords reads "There is no god but Allah" and below the swords it reads "Mohammed is His Messenger"

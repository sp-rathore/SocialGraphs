The monument is 40-meter high, made of titanium. It was completed in the USSR on July 4, 1980. Sculptor: Pavel Bondarenko, architects: Ya.B.Belopolsky, F.M.Gazhevsky, constructor: A.F.Sudakov. Uploaded from the Russian Wiki ( ). Geographic coordinates of the monument: 55°42′29.62″N, 37°34′56.84″E

Summary

Diagram of where Rosa Parks sat on the board Cleveland Avenue bus, 6 p.m., Thursday, December 1, 1955.

Source: Civil Case 1147 Browder, et al v. Gayle, et. al; U.S. District Court for Middle District of Alabama, Northern (Montgomery) Division Record Group 21: Records of the District Court of the United States National Archives and Records Administration-Southeast Region, East Point, GA.

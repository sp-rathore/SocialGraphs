Summary

The flag of Bulgaria. The colors are specified at http://www.government.bg/cgi-bin/e-cms/vis/vis.pl?s=001&p=0034&n=000005&g= as:

  • White: Pantone Safe
  • Green: 17-5936 (Green TC) [textile colour]
  • Red: 18-1664 (Fiery Red TC)

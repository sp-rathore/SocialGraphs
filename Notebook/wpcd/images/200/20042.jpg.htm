Summary

Carolus Linnaeus

This image is a version of Image:Carl_von_Linné.jpg on Wikipedia Commons. This is a reproduction of a painting by Alexander Roslin in 1775. The original painting can be viewed at the Royal Science Academy of Sweden (Kungliga vetenskapsakademin).

This particular version has had dust and missing specs of paint deleted. The contributor grants a GNU Free Documentation License for this cleaned-up version.

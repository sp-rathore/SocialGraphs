Johann Sebastian Bach

Portrait by Elias Gottlob Haussmann, Circa 1748.

This painting is actually the second version painted by Haussman. This painting is owned by Mr. William Scheide of Princeton, New Jersey.

Source: http://www.jsbach.net/images/jsbach2.html 250251138250251138

Other version: Image:Bach.jpg (on Commons)

The two-dimensional work of art depicted in this image is in the public domain in the United States and in those countries with a copyright term of life of the author plus 100 years. This photograph of the work is also in the public domain in the United States (see Bridgeman Art Library v. Corel Corp.).

The flag of the United States.

  • 50 white stars on a blue field, 13 red and white stripes.
  • In use since 4 July 1960, when Hawaii was added as the 50th US state.
  • Designed by Robert Heft in 1958
  • Specifications: United States Executive Order 10834
  • Proportions: 1.9 × 1.0
  • Colors:
    • Cable No. 70180 Old Glory Red ( PMS 193C)
    • Cable No. 70001 White
    • Cable No. 70075 Old Glory Blue (PMS 281C)
  • License: public domain

Image notes

  • The geometric specifications for the union jack and the sizes and positioning of the stars are given in an attachment to Executive Order 10834 of 21 August 1959, printed in 3 CFR, 1959–1963 Comp., p. 368.

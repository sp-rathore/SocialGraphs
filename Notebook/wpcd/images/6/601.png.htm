Summary

The national flag of Thailand has 3 colours:

  • red, which stands for the blood spilt to maintain Thailand's independence, or often more simply described as representing Thai people,
  • white, which stands for Buddhism, the predominant religion of the country and
  • blue, which stands for the Monarchy of Thailand.

Coat of Arms for the Government of Northern Ireland, granted in 1925. The grant has not been rescinded, but these arms are historical rather than Semiofficial, as the body to which the arms were granted no longer exists and so cannot be used unless regranted to another armiger. The White Stag bears the colours of the province of Ulster and the lion, the harp banner for Ireland.

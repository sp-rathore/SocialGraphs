This image was selected as picture of the day for May 28, 2006.

Taken by Wing-Chi Poon on 1st July 2005 in Jasper National Park, Alberta, Canada (along Yellowhead Highway 16 between intersection to Malign Valley Road and intersection to Snaring River Campground, overlooking Colin Range in the south). This photo shows the place where the rainbow rises (the end of a rainbow), many mythologies were associated with such places.

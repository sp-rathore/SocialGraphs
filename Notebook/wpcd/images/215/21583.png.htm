This image shows a registered coat of arms from Sweden. Even if the design is free to use (per the license), it is not permitted to use in such a way that implies it is being supported by the municipality or county, respecively, for example in part of trademarks or other commercial products. This requires permission of the municipality or county council. According to laws SFS 1970:498 and 1976:100.
Denna bild visar en registrerad svensk sv:vapensköld.

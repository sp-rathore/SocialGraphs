Caption

Official Photographic Portrait of Her Majesty The Queen of Canada

  • Queen of Canada - Wearing the insignia of the Sovereign of the Order of Canada (above) & Sovereign of the Order of Military Merit (below)
  • The official Canadian portrait of Her Majesty Queen Elizabeth II was taken during the 2002 Jubilee Visit and unveiled on June 2, 2003, in honour of the 50th anniversary of her coronation (without border/frame).

Tomb of Richard I of England at Fontevraud Abbey near Chinon, in Anjou, France. (Photographed July 2003)

Richard died at Le Château de Châlus Chabrol in Châlus of a crossbow wound. His entrails were buried at the château while his heart was taken to Rouen and the rest of the body to Fontevraud.

Summary

This is a 19th century fictional account of the death of Queen Elizabeth I depicting her successor, King James VI of Scotland accepting her proclamation of his accession to the throne, effectively becoming King James I of England and the first rulers of England of the House of Stuart which would last until the death of Queen Anne in 1714.

This image was selected as picture of the day for June 24, 2006.

Jupiter as seen by the space probe "Cassini". This is the most detailed global colour portrait of Jupiter ever assembled. It is produced from several high resolution images taken a little more than a day before Cassini's closest approach to Jupiter.

(Image credit: NASA/JPL/Space Science Institute)

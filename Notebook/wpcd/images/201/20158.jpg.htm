Description
English: Picture of the Formation lap for the 1996 Monaco Grand Prix, pictured from the front to back are: Mika Häkkinen ( McLaren), Heinz-Harald Frentzen ( Sauber), Jacques Villeneuve ( Williams), Mika Salo ( Tyrrell), Jos Verstappen ( Footwork), Johnny Herbert ( Sauber). The hidden car behind Johnny Herbert is the Ligier of eventual race winner Olivier Panis.

Summary

The 100th plate from Ernst Haeckel's Kunstformen der Natur (1904), depicting organisms classified as Antilopina.

Species:

  1. Tetracerus quadricornis
    • en: Four-horned Antelope
  2. Catoblepas gnu
  3. Tragelaphus spekeii gratus = Tragelaphus gratus
    • en: Sitatunga, de: Wasserkudu
  4. Antilocapra americana = Antilope furcifera
    • en: Pronghorn
  5. Antilope ellipsiprymna = Cervicapra ellipsiprymna
  6. Hippotragus niger
    • en: Sable Antelope
  7. Addax nasomaculatus
    • en: Addax
  8. Tragelaphus strepsiceros = Tragelaphus kudu = Antilope strepsiceros
    • en: Greater Kudu
  9. Tragelaphus scriptus = Antilope masculata
    • en: Bushbuck

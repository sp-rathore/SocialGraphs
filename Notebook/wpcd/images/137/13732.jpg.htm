Summary

The ceremonial court of the bath-gymnasium complex at the archaeological site of Sardis, near present-day Sart in the Manisa province of Turkey. Dated to 211 - 212 AD. Photo taken by User:AtilimGunesBaydin, on February 1, 2003.

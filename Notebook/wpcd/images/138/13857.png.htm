This is the flag of Louisiana, from the xrmap flag collection 2.7. On 25 May 2006, a bill was approved in Louisiana that would add three drops of blood to the flag and the state seal. The three drops symbolise what the mother pelican does to herself to make sure the young pelican have food if no other source is found.

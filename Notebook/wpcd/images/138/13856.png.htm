The flag of Kentucky is a navy blueish colour with a golden symbol that has the words "Diveded We Fall" and "United We Stand".Also, in gold above the symbol it says "Common Wealth Of Kentucky".

Flag of Kentucky, from the xrmap flag collection 2.7.

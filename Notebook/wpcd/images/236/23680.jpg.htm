Malmédy massacre - approximately 70 members of Battery B killed after captured . Fair J. Bryant was travelling with Battery A of 285th at time of massacre. Occurred at beginning of Battle of the Bulge

  • Image ID: theb2930, Historic C&GS Collection
  • Location: Malmedy, Belgium
  • Photo Date: December 17, 1944

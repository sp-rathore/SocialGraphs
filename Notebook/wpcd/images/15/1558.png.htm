The meaning of the colours:

Yellow  : represents the country's minerals
Black  : is for black majority
White  : is for peace (NOT white minority)
Red  : represents blood shed in the civil war (chimurenga)
Green  : is for vegetation (agricultural wealth)
The bird : is the zimbabwean bird

en: Statue of the Libertador of the Plaza Bolívar in Caracas

  • Place - Plaza Bolivar. Primigenia Catedral parish, Libertador municipality, Capital District ( Caracas), Venezuela.
  • Date - July 1, 2004.
  • Photographer - Kinori.
  • An ecuestrian statue of Simón Bolívar is placed in the centre of the plaza. A bird rests on the head of the Liberator Bolivar.

A picture of the 2004 tsunami in Thailand. Taken and made Public Domain by David Rydevik (email: skylark292 at gmail dot com ), Stockholm, Sweden. According to David Rydevik (via email) the picture was taken at Ao Nang, Thailand.

Originally at sv:Bild:Davidsvågfoto.JPG.

Summary

Richard Burton's tomb in Mortlake, London. A statement releasing the pictures for use on wikipedia is below.

(The site referred to is www.burtonfund.org).

You are free to use any pictures on the site except 3. These are as follows:

No1. - Entry Page Picture of Richard and Isabel (copyright Mary Lovell who I believe got authorization from Orlean House in the UK)) No 3 of slide show - Copyright is Rowena Hart No 4 of slide show - Copyright is Rowena Hart

All other pictures(i.e. slide show 1, 2, 5, 6, etc) as well as the picture archive at the end of the slide show (approx 20) you may use as you see fit.

Best wishes,

Bernie Lauser BURTON TOMB RESTORATION FUND

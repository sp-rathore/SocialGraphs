Description

Crowned flag of the Kingdom of Italy (1861-1946), alternative to Flag of Italy (without crown). In a governmental or a military context, the crowned version was always used.

  • Flag of The Regio Esercito (Italian Army until 1946)
  • Flag of the Regia Marina (Italian Navy until 1946)

This graph shows that oil production has already peaked in non- OPEC, non-former Soviet Union countries.

Source

From public domain document "Strategic Significance of America's Oil Shale Resource Volume I Assessment of Strategic Issues" at http://www.fe.doe.gov/programs/reserves/publications/Pubs-NPR/npr_strategic_significancev1.pdf

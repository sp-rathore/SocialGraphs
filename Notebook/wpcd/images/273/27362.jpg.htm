Summary

Lieutenant General Philip Neame (centre), Lieutenant General Richard O'Connor (centre, middle distance), Major General Gambier-Parry (right) and Brigadier Coombe (left) following their capture by the Germans, 6 April 1941. A Junkers Ju 52 is in the background.

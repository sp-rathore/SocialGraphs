A new dome started growing on October 18,1980. This October dome was 112 feet (34 meters) high and 985 feet (300 meters) wide, making it taller than a nine-story building and wider than the length of three football fields. This aerial view is from the north. (October 24, 1980, by Terry A. Leighley, USGS/Sandia Labs)

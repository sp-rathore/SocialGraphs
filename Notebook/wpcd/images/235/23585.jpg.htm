Japanese cruiser Oyodo comes alongside the damaged Japanese aircraft carrier Zuikaku, from which this photo was taken, for Admiral Jisaburo Ozawa to transfer his flag in the battle off Cape Engaño, October 25, 1944. Notice the angle of the flight deck with respect to the sea - Zuikaku had developed quite a list by this time. Donated to the US Navy Historical Centre by Kazutoshi Hando. source

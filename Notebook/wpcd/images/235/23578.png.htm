Description

This flag is the Union Flag in the 3:5 ratio exclusivly used by the British Army. Otherwise, the correct ratio is but 1:2. This image is conformant to commonly accepted construction rules; the "corners" on the top right hand and bottom left hand red diagonals are intentional (see the link, s. "Other ratios" for discussion.)

Main fleet action at the battle of Jutland, 17:30–21:00 31 May 1916

The British were in six columns led by (from NE to SW at ❶) King George V, Orion, Iron Duke, Benbow, Colossus and Marlborough. Approximate times are ❶ 18:00, ❷ 18:15, ❸ 18:30, ❹ 19:00, ❺ 19:15, ❻ 20:00, ❼ 21:00.

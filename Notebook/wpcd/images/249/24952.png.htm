Summary

en: Unofficial Red ensign for Hong Kong used prior to the Handover to the PRC in 1997. According to FOTW, this ensign was used on merchant ships for a short time in the late 1980's. This image is based on User:David Newton's red ensign; the badge is from the author of xrmap.

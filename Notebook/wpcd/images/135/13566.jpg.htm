Summary

This diagram of the Tower of London appears in Picturesque England by Laura Valentine (1891), where it is stated to be based on an engraving published in 1597.

Copyright: Laura Valentine does not appear in the Dictionary of National Biography, and I have not been able to determine her date of death, but it is probable that she died more than 70 years ago, making this image public domain in the UK.

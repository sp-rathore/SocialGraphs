Snowy Rowles, photographed by Arthur Upfield

http://nla.gov.au/nla.pic-an9897467-2 : Rowles standing beside James Ryan's car [picture]; Part of Collection of photographs by Arthur Upfield of the 'Murchison Bones Case' scene, and a portrait [picture]

Snowy Rowles was convicted of the murder of James Ryan. He disposed of Ryan's body using methods similar to those of a murderer in Upfield's 1931 novel The Sands of Windee.

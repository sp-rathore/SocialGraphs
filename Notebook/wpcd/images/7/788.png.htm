Summary

The Flag of Jamaica.

Slovenščina: državna zastava Jamajke

“The Sun shineth, the land is green, and the people are strong and creative” is the symbolism of the colours of the flag. BLACK represents the strength and creativity of the people; GREEN represents hope and agricultural resources; GOLD represents the natural wealth and beauty of sunlight.

Source: Drawn by User:SKopp

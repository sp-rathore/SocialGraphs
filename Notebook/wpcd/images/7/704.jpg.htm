Permission

"I believe the work is anonymous because those are photographs collected by Abel Paz from various CNT militants who donated their personal stuff to him over the years, and are currently held by the Fundación Anselmo Lorenzo in Barcelona. They were widely republished, so you can get them from almost anywhere, but the main source is him and his books, which never refer to the original photographers. Not even him knows. You can check this on Paz, A. (1976) Durruti: The People Armed. Montréal: Black Rose Books, or V/A (1996) Durruti 1896-1936. London: Active/Beastie. Mr.Rocks 04:02, 6 January 2007 (UTC)"

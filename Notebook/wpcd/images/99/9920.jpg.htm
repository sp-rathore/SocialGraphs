Summary

Español: Estela de los Tres Zapotes.

This is a drawing of the back of Stela C from the archaelogical site of Tres Zapotes. This is artifact is particularly notable because it is one of the few set of glyphs in the Epi-Olmec script and because it is one of the earliest examples of the Mesoamerican Long Date calendar system.

The Vatican City State released this photo into the public domain, one of few that have been done so, of the President of the United States and two former Presidents of the United States paying homage to Pope John Paul II lying in state at St. Peter's Basilica. The photo was released through L'Osservatore Romano on April 6, 2005. From left to right in the forefront: Laura Bush, George W. Bush, George H.W. Bush, Bill Clinton, Condoleezza Rice, and Andrew Card.

This file is in the public domain, because the photo was released in to the public domain by the Vatican City State.

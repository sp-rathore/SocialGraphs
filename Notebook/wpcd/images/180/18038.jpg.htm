Description

Air National Guard members deliver water and food to stranded citizens here after the city was devastated by Hurricane Katrina. The National Guard has been mobilized to take part in Joint Task Force - Katrina, a humanitarian assistance operation.

(U.S. Navy photo by Photographer's Mate First Class Brien Aho)

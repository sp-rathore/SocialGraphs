Summary

Español: Una explosión nuclear ilumina la noche durante una prueba en los Estados Unidos.

The XX-34 BADGER explosion on April 18, 1953, as part of Operation Upshot-Knothole, at the Nevada Test Site.

The photo is from the Department of Energy, Nevada Site Office's Photo Library - Atmospheric, specifically XX34.JPG.

Alternative source: http://www.nv.doe.gov/library/photos/photodetails.aspx?ID=1048

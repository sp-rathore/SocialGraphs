Description

USS Wisconsin fires her big guns during the 1991 Gulf War. ID: DNST9208524 Service Depicted: Navy The battleship USS WISCONSIN (BB-64) fires a round from one of the Mark 7 16-inch/50-caliber guns in its No. 3 turret during Operation Desert Storm. The ship's target is an Iraqi artillery battery in southern Kuwaiti.

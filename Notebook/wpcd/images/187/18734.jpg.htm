My 1998 Harley Davidson FXST-C (Softail Custom). Picture taken on the roof of a parking garage in Downtown Dallas. Front wheel is not standard, the wheel in the picture is off a Heritage Softail. FXSTCs are delivered with a standard 21" x 3" front wheel.

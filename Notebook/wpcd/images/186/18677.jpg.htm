Summary

Image of Indonesian President Suharto attending 1970 Non-Aligned Movement meeting in Lusaka. Cropped from a larger photo found in Suharto: A Political Biography by R.E. Elson, published 2001 by Cambridge University Press.

Photo is attributed to State Secretariat of the Republic of Indonesia. As a work of a government body, this is a public domain work.

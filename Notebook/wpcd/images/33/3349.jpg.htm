Description

Photo of the comet Hale-Bopp above a tree. This picture was taken in the vicinity of Pazin in Istria/Croatia during a short easter holiday. The tree was illuminated using a small flashlight. To the lower right of the comet the Andromeda Galaxy M31 is also faintly visible. Technical information regard this photo:

  • Lens: Nikon 35mm f/2
  • Film: Kodak Royal Gold 400
  • Exposure: 2 min at f/2

Description

USS Iowa (BB-61) fires a full broadside of nine 16"/50 and six 5"/38 guns during a target exercise near Vieques Island, Puerto Rico ( 21° N 65° W).
Note concussion effects on the water surface, and 16-inch gun barrels in varying degrees of recoil.

I produced this map myself using the program here and modifying it. It remains in the public domain. The map is an azimuthal equal-area projection. The Line of Control through Kashmir is green. Where the line of control along the Indo-Chinese frontier coincides with one side's territorial claims, the line is pink; borders claimed but not controlled are purple. Non-disputed boudaries are in red. QuartierLatin1968 01:36, 21 May 2005 (UTC)

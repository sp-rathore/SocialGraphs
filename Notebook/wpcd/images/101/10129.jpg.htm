The Palace of Westminster at night seen from the south bank of the River Thames.

Victoria Tower and the House of Lords is on the left. The Clock Tower of Big Ben and the House of Commons is on the right. The spire left of centre is the 300ft ventilation chimney above the central lobby.

The twin towers with flag just visible in the background is Westminster Abbey.

This image displays an insignia of a rank in the Bundeswehr. International law requires these for combatant identification which therefore makes copyrighting rank insignias in violation of international law. This means the image is in the public domain. This applies worldwide.

Note that the use of such symbols is restricted in many countries independently of the copyright status.
This image is in the public domain according to German copyright law because it is part of a statute, ordinance, official decree or judgment ( official work) issued by a German federal or state authority or court ( § 5 Abs.1 UrhG).

This is a picture that was taken about 50 kilometers from Bishkek, I think it is called "Al-aksa range", a national park. It is beautiful, nobody goes there since nobody has a car(virtually) so no Coke cans and no sign of human life. At night you can see the stars so brightly... (there is no light contamination, since Bishkek has about 100 public lights that work, and a lot more that don't) I took the picture myself (and scanned it in).

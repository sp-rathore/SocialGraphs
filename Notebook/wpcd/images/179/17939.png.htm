Summary

English: The flag of India.
Deutsch: Flagge Indiens.

Español: Bandera de la India

Walon : Drapea d' l' Inde
Français : Drapeau de l'Inde

The colours are saffron, white and green. The navy blue wheel in the centre of the flag has a diameter approximately the width of the white band and is called Ashoka's Dharma Chakra, with 24 spokes (after Ashoka, the Great). Each spoke depicts one hour of the day, portraying the prevalence of righteousness all 24 hours of it.

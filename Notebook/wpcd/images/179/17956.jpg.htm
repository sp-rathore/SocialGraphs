Chesapeake and Ohio Railroad EMD F7 diesel locomotive #7086, photographed at Handley, West Virginia, July 9, 1953. This locomotive, built October 1952, is a late Phase II example and representative of late F7 production.


This image (call number OP-2939) is from the collection of the photographs of the late Otto Perry (b. 1894, d. 1970) held at the Western History Department of the Denver Public Library ( http://photoswest.org/), and is copyrighted.

David Garrick in the role of Richard III, in Colley Cibber's adaptation of Shakespeare's play, painted in 1745 (but acted in 1743) by William Hogarth. Oil on canvas, original in the Walker gallery, Liverpool. Downloaded from http://www.liverpoolmuseums.org.uk/walker/collections/essays/rococo.asp.

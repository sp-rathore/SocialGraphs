9th century fresco from the Bezeklik grottoes (uighurian: "Place, where images are", chinese: Qian Fo Dong - "Thousand Buddhas Caves") near Turfan, Tarim Basin, China.

The person to the left is a european/western asian trader or monk (distinguishable by his red hair and full beard and face), to the right an asian buddhist monk.

B-52 Stratofortress with payloads it was capable of carrying.

"The B-52G of the 2d Bomb Wing, Barksdale AFB, Louisiana, "poses" with examples of the various munitions it was capable of carrying, 17 March 1982 (left to right: B-43, B-61/1, B-28, B-53)."

Ko-hyoteki_Sydney.jpg‎ (450 × 324 pixel, file size: 65 KB, MIME type: image/jpeg)

June 1, 1942. A Japanese Ko-hyoteki class midget submarine, believed to be the vessel known as Midget No. 14, is raised from the bed of Sydney Harbour. The night before the picture was taken, the submarine's two crew members were part of a raid on shipping in Sydney Harbour. Photograph by Ronald Noel Keam.

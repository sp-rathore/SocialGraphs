040220-N-3874J-004 San Diego, Calif. (Feb. 20, 2004) – Military service members and their families from the around the San Diego area, watch as Eugene “Killer” Edgerson of the “world-famous” Harlem Globetrotters go for the slam-dunk over the heads of the hapless New York Nationals at the San Diego Sports Arena. The “Ambassadors of Goodwill” have been entertaining audiences for over 75 years. Famous Globetrotters such as Wilt Chamberlain, Curly Neal, and Meadowlark Lemon have performed for more than 100 million fans in 115 countries. U.S. Navy photo by Photographer’s Mate 2nd Class Daniel A. Jones. (RELEASED)

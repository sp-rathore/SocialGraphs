Representation of Mestizos during the Latin American colonial period. An example of pintura de castas, that classified people by their ancestry.

Paint by Miguel Cabrera, mexican painter, XVIII.

Text

1. De español e india produce mestizo.

español 1. india 2. mestizo 3.

"1. From Spaniard and Amerindian woman, begets Mestizo."

  1. Spaniard.
  2. Amerindian woman.
  3. Mestizo.

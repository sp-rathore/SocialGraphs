Subject: 21 APEC leaders wearing chamantos, a traditional Chilean poncho, during the APEC 2004 summit held in Santiago de Chile. Image taken at the Palacio de La Moneda.

From left to right: President Vicente Fox of Mexico, Prime Minister Abdullah Ahmad Badawi of Malaysia, President Susilo Bambang Yudhoyono of Indonesia, Chief Executive Tung Chee-hwa of Hong Kong SAR, Prime Minister Thaksin Shinawatra of Thailand, Special Envoy Yuan T. Lee of Chinese Taipei (Taiwan), Sultan Hassanal Bolkiah of Brunei, Prime Minister John Howard of Australia, Prime Minister Helen Clark of New Zealand, President George W. Bush of the United States, President Ricardo Lagos of Chile, President Tran Duc Luong of Vietnam, President Gloria Macapagal-Arroyo of the Philippines, Prime Minister Paul Martin of Canada, President Hu Jintao of the People's Republic of China, President Vladimir Putin of Russia, Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong of Singapore, Prime Minister Junichiro Koizumi of Japan, President Roh Moo-hyun of South Korea, Prime Minister Sir Michael Somare of Papua New Guinea, and President Alejandro Toledo of Peru.

Source: http://www.apec2004.cl/galeria/AELM29gr.jpg (Chilean government site)

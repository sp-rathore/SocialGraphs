Summary

This gel was created and imaged by me.

Expression of a specific gene in three seperate tissues was tested by two primers. As the gel shows, Tissue #1 lacks that gene, whereas Tissue #2 and #3 posses it. A positive control was used to determine that the PCR conditions were adequate and a 1kb ladder was used to determine the size of the bands.

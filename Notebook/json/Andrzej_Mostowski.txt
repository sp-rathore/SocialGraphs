Marek]<br>[[Moshé Machover]]<br>[[Helena Rasiowa]]<br>[[Roman Sikorski]]<br>[[Paweł Zbierski]]\n| known_for         = \n| awards            = \n}}\n'''Andrzej Mostowski''' (1 November 1913 – 22 August 1975) was a [[Poland|Polish]] [[mathematician]]. He is perhaps best remembered for the [[Mostowski collapse lemma]].\n\nBorn in [[Lviv|Lemberg]], [[Austria-Hungary]], Mostowski entered [[University of Warsaw]] in 1931. He was influenced by [[Kazimierz Kuratowski|Kuratowski]], [[Adolf Lindenbaum|Lindenbaum]] and [[Alfred Tarski|Tarski]]. His Ph.D. came in 1939, officially directed by [[Kazimierz Kuratowski|Kuratowski]] but in practice directed by [[Alfred Tarski|Tarski]] who was a young lecturer at that time.\n\nHe became an accountant after [[Invasion of Poland (1939)|the German invasion of Poland]] but continued working in the [[Underground education in Poland during World War II|Underground Warsaw University]]. After the [[Warsaw uprising]] of 1944 the [[Nazis]] tried to put him in a concentration camp. With the help of some Polish nurses he escaped to a hospital, choosing to take bread with him rather than his notebook containing his research. Some of this research he reconstructed after the War, however much of it remained lost.\n\nThis work was largely on [[recursion theory]] and [[Undecidable problem|undecidability]]. From 1946 until his death  in [[Vancouver]], [[Canada]], he worked at the [[University of Warsaw]]. Much of work during that time was on first order [[logic]] and [[model theory]].\n\nHis son [[Tadeusz Mostowski|Tadeusz]] is also a mathematician working on [[differential geometry]].<ref>http://www.mimuw.edu.pl/wydzial/organizacja/pracownicy/tadeusz.mostowski.xml?LANG=en&para=&parb=</ref> With [[Krzysztof Kurdyka]] and [[Adam Parusinski]], Tadeusz Mostowski solved [[René Thom]]'s [[gradient conjecture]] in 2000.\n\n==See also==\n*[[List of Poles#Mathematics|List of Polish People]]\n*[[Mostowski model]]\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n\n==Bibliography==\n\n=== Books by Mostowski ===\n\n*{{citation|mr=0485384\n|last=Kuratowski|first= Kazimierz|last2= Mostowski|first2= Andrzej\n|title=Set theory.

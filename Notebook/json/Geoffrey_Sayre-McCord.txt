He has written extensively in these areas.  He is known especially for his work on [[moral realism]] and on [[David Hume]]'s moral theory.  He has also written on [[contractualism]]. His ''Essays on Moral Realism'' is widely used in undergraduate and graduate courses on meta-ethics and he was, for five years, a co-editor of the highly regarded journal ''[[Noûs]]''.     \nSayre-McCord received his BA from [[Oberlin College]] and his PhD (under the direction of [[David Gauthier]]) from the [[University of Pittsburgh]]. The recipient of several university-wide teaching awards, Sayre-McCord is the Morehead-Cain Alumni Distinguished Professor of Philosophy and Director of the ''Philosophy, Politics, and Economics

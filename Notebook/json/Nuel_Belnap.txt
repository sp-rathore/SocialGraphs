He taught at the [[University of Pittsburgh]] since 1963 until his retirement in 2011; before that he was at [[Yale University]]. His best-known work is his collaboration with [[Alan Ross Anderson]] on [[relevance logic]]. He is also co-author with Ming Xu and Michel Perloff of ''Facing the Future''.
